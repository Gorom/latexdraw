LaTeXDraw 1.7 New and Noteworthy



Here are some of the more noteworthy things available in LaTeXDraw 1.7. See the release note for details about bugs fixed and other changes. Here the roadmap page of LaTeXDraw 1.7.



Shadow



Now, a shape can have a shadow; a shape is defined by:
  • its color;
  • its size;
  • its angle.


Colours shifting



Two colours can be used to fill a shape; the shifting is defined by:
  • its angle;
  • its position.




Framed box



A text can be boxed by one or several boxes.





Improvements


Several improvements are that:
  • a text can be opaque;
  • polygon can have double line;
  • the frames of parameters have a better interface;
  • can change the size of the round corner (for rectangle and square).
